Questions

Everything clients usually ask before starting.

How long does a website take?

A simple business website can usually be completed faster than an e-commerce or custom system. The final timeline depends on the number of pages, how quickly content is supplied and the revision requirements.

What content do I need to provide?

Your business name, services, contact information, product details, prices, images and any examples you like. I can help organise and improve the wording, but original business information must come from you.

How many revisions are included?

The starter packages include one organised revision round. Additional rounds or major changes outside the agreed scope can be quoted separately.

Are domain and hosting included?

No. Domain and hosting are separate so that they stay under the client’s ownership. I can guide you through purchasing and connecting them.

Can you add online payments?

Yes, where the payment provider supports the business and approves the account. Gateway fees, provider charges and any paid plugins are separate from the development price.

Can you maintain the website after launch?

Yes. Product uploads, text updates, banners, bug fixes and future improvements can be handled as separate maintenance work.
